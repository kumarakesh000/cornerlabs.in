How It Works

From raw camera feed to identified species in milliseconds. Our pipeline is built for speed and accuracy in the wild.

01
📷
Capture Feed

Camera traps or live feeds stream footage from forest zones around the clock. Video is processed frame-by-frame in real time.

02
🧠
AI Detection

A computer vision model scans each frame, drawing bounding boxes around detected animals and assigning confidence scores.

03
📊
Live Dashboard

Detection data flows instantly to this dashboard — species name, confidence level, timestamp, camera zone, and threat status.
