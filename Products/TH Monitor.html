What is a TH Monitor?

A Temperature and Humidity (TH) Monitor is used to continuously measure and maintain controlled environmental conditions, especially in cold storage applications,data warehouse etc.

The TH Monitor ensures stable temperature and humidity, providing real-time readings, alerts, and data logging to prevent spoilage, protect equipment, and maintain quality and compliance across industrial and commercial environments.
