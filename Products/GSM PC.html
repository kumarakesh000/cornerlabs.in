GSM Pump Controller Overview

Control your water pumps remotely with advanced GSM technology

A GSM Pump Controller is a device that allows you to operate and monitor a water pump using a mobile phone. It uses a GSM (mobile network) module to send and receive commands through calls or SMS.

Pump ON/OFF

Remotely start or stop your water pump with a simple phone call or SMS command from anywhere.

Schedule Operation

Set automatic ON/OFF times for your pump to operate on a predefined schedule without manual intervention.

Status Updates

Receive real-time status updates about your pump operation.
